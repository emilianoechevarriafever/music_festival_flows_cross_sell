Description

Polar Sound Occident Festival has been surprising audiences every year since 2019, bringing together the hottest artists of the moment for live performances. A unique location on the peninsula, with the best ski slopes during the day and the best music in the afternoon and evening, creates the perfect formula for enjoying one of the best festivals of the season.
